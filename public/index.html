Dr. Ellen McMahon has worked in inpatient and outpatient facilities with both adults and adolescents with a wide range of diagnoses. Ellen provides an environment that allows her clients to feel safe to share their stories and receive the therapeutic interventions necessary to heal, grow, and meet life’s challenges. Ellen utilizes multiple therapies depending on the clients needs. She looks at how past experiences, thought patterns, and family systems influence internal beliefs and relationships in the here and now. She encourages clients to set achievable, relevant goals that promote healing, growth and healthy relationships.
Specialties
Depression Anxiety PTSD Bipolar Couples Relationships Divorce Domestic Violence Spirituality Self-Esteem Life Transitions Premarital Parenting Boundaries Codependency Faith-Based.
Treatment Approach
Cognitive Behavioral (CBT), Brief Solution Focused Therapy (BSFT), Gottman Couples Counseling, Eye Movement Desensitization and Reprocessing (EMDR).
Client Focus
Adults (18+) Individual Couples Groups.
Dr. Ellen McMahon has a wide range of knowledge and life experiences, as well as, a dedication to Christian service. She provides her clients with quality care in a compassionate, safe environment that promotes healing and growth as they meet life’s many challenges.
